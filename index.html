Housing Affordability in Melbourne

Exploring how rent prices and incomes vary across Melbourne’s suburbs

Melbourne’s housing market reflects a complex balance between income levels and living costs across its suburbs. While some areas boast higher wages that align with rising rents, others reveal a growing affordability gap. Key Insights

  • Median rent in Melbourne has steadily increased over the past 5 years.
  • Inner-city areas show higher rent growth compared to outer suburbs.
  • Rental affordability is becoming more challenging for low to mid-income households.
  • Seasonal variations are noticeable, with rents peaking around summer months.
